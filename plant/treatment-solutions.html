Treatment Solutions

Organic and chemical treatments for common plant diseases

Organic Treatments

Neem Oil Spray

Mix 5ml neem oil with 1L water. Effective against aphids, powdery mildew.

Garlic Extract

Crush 10 cloves in 1L water, steep overnight. Natural fungicide.

Baking Soda Solution

1 tsp baking soda + 1L water + few drops soap. For mildew control.

Chemical Treatments

Copper Fungicide

Effective against blight and bacterial spots. Apply every 7-10 days.

Sulfur-based Spray

Controls powdery mildew and rust. Follow label instructions.

Systemic Fungicide

For severe infections. Absorbed by plant tissue.

Preventive Measures

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops yearly to prevent soil-borne diseases.

Proper Spacing

Ensure adequate air circulation between plants.

Morning Watering

Water at base, avoid wetting leaves.
